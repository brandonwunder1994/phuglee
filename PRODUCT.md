# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

Primary: solo wholesaler operators running the full Collect → Filter → Analyze pipeline on distressed property leads. They work long sessions at a desk, often at night, with real money on the line per dial.

Secondary (future): paying customers with their own logins and scoped data. Not the Phase 3 build target yet.

## Product Purpose

Phuglee Distress OS helps wholesalers source leads at the clerk, scrub city violation data, rank visible distress from Street View, and export dial-ready lists before aggregators recycle the same names.

Success looks like: one product that feels like a single war room, not six stitched apps; operators trust the UI; the pipeline runs without freezes; the site looks intentional, not AI-generated.

## Positioning

The #1 distressed lead source for wholesalers who do the work at the clerk, not the red ocean of recycled aggregator lists.

## Brand Personality

Gritty field ops. Confident. No hype. Operators first.

Voice: plain, direct, ops slang ("hit the clerk," "scrub the mess," "dial-ready"). Not corporate SaaS, not cyberpunk HUD, not generic startup dashboard.

## Anti-references

- Generic AI SaaS: purple gradients, glass cards everywhere, hero metric templates, identical icon+heading+text card grids
- Cyber HUD chrome: scanlines, reticles, "NO SIGNAL," fake terminal aesthetics on Analyze
- Cream/sand body backgrounds with timid gray text (reads as 2026 AI default)
- Fake product surfaces: Vault showing frosted mock leads as if they are real data
- Numbered section eyebrows (01 / 02 / 03) on every block
- Em-dash-heavy marketing copy cadence

## Design Principles

1. **One weapon, one look** — Collect, Filter, Analyze, Forge, and shell pages share the same tokens, buttons, and type scale.
2. **Work-first** — Dominant task at first paint; marketing chrome is secondary inside the app.
3. **Proof over decoration** — Real property imagery, real scan buckets, real kill counts; never decorative fake metrics.
4. **Restraint with heat** — Dark earth surfaces, cream headlines, ember/gold only where action or success lives.
5. **Do not break the pipeline** — Visual passes must not change scoring, bucket logic, brain training rules, or saved operator data.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA target for text contrast on dark surfaces. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` on all theater/feed animations. Phase 3 is visual-only; real multi-user auth is a later milestone.
