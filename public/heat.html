Where the heat comes from

These aren't cold lists scraped off a portal. They're real distress signals pulled from public records — code violations, water shutoffs for delinquency, probates that need to sell, foreclosures facing auction with a strict timeline, tax liens, and upcoming tax sales.

Instead of manually hunting through tens of thousands of records hoping someone wants to sell, we hand you properties that are already in distress for a variety of reasons. We provide the leads — closing them is on you — but we get you dramatically closer to a deal than starting from scratch.

The playbook

Three steps. Zero guesswork.

01

Collect leads at the source

Request public records directly from cities and counties. While data aggregators make you wait weeks for stale lists everyone else is buying, you're getting fresh leads right from the source — your edge on the competition starts here.

Start Collecting
02

Scrub with Filter

When the city sends data back, drop it in — Excel, CSV, PDF, photos, whatever they throw at you. Filter normalizes it into a clean spreadsheet: address, city, state, zip, violation type, dates, and more.

Long descriptions? We read through them and cut anything that isn't a real distress signal. What's left is a scrubbed list of actual opportunities — ready to filter and skip trace.

03

Analyze with AI — verified by humans

Take your refined list and import it into whatever software you normally use for real estate data. Apply your filters, skip trace, then reimport into Analyze.

Our AI reviews each property and classifies distress level — then a second layer of human review double-checks the AI. AI is incredible, but it can't be the sole source of truth when you need premium lead flow and deals that haven't officially hit the market yet.
